01

Overview

Discover Movement Connect is a mobile application designed to unify scheduling, coaching, and client communication for a functional strength and pain management gym. The app integrates with the gym's existing scheduling and membership platform and replaces a third-party coaching tool as the primary method for delivering personalized training programs.

The project focused on reducing administrative friction for the coaching staff while giving clients a seamless, centralized experience for managing their training, booking sessions, and tracking progress.

02

Problem Statement

Coaches and clients rely on a patchwork of disconnected tools for scheduling, program delivery, and communication. Programs are delivered through one platform, scheduling through another, and communication through texts and email. This fragmentation leads to lost information, duplicated effort, and inconsistent client experiences.

The goal was to design a unified mobile experience that brings scheduling, coaching, and client communication into a single platform, keeping data synced with the scheduling platform automatically so the coaching staff can focus on what they do best.

03

Research

Research began with stakeholder interviews, workflow observation, and a review of the tools already in use. Key methods included:

  • In-depth interviews with the head coach to understand daily workflows and pain points
  • Client feedback sessions to identify friction in booking, communication, and program access
  • Competitive analysis of existing scheduling and coaching platforms to identify integration gaps and feature overlap
  • Workflow mapping to visualize how information flows between coach, client, and platform

Key Pain Points

1
Fragmented Tools

Coaches manage clients across multiple disconnected platforms, leading to duplicated work and lost context.

2
Progress Visibility

Clients lack a clear, centralized view of their progress, making it hard to stay motivated and track improvement.

3
Scattered Communication

Messages, updates, and program instructions are spread across texts, email, and multiple apps.

4
Program Delivery

Delivering and updating personalized training programs requires manual effort across disconnected systems.

04

Personas

Two primary personas were developed to represent the core user types: the coach/business owner and the client.

The Coach — Head Coach & PT

As the gym owner and head coach, he needs a unified tool to manage clients, deliver programs, and keep scheduling synced without juggling disconnected platforms.

Sarah — Client

As a busy professional focused on mobility and strength, Sarah needs a simple, accessible way to follow her training program and track progress without added complexity.
